Our Story

From Seoul to
the World.

Founded in 2004 with a single vision — to prove that premium aviation could originate from Asia and rival the world's finest carriers. VINUS Air launched its inaugural service from Seoul Incheon International Airport with three aircraft and a crew of 200.

Two decades later, we operate one of Asia-Pacific's most awarded fleets, connecting 180 destinations across six continents. Our commitment has never wavered: every passenger, every flight, every time.
